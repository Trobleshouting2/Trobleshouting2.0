<p><strong>Nutrition Month</strong> is celebrated every <mark>July</mark> in the Philippines to encourage everyone to eat <b>healthy foods</b> and live an <em>active lifestyle</em>. It reminds people that good nutrition helps us stay <u>strong</u>, healthy, and ready to learn every day.</p>

<h2>Why Healthy Eating is Important</h2>

<p>Eating a balanced diet gives our body the <strong>energy</strong> it needs. Fruits and vegetables provide <mark>vitamins</mark> and minerals that help protect us from diseases.</p>

<p>Drinking enough water, exercising regularly, and avoiding too much junk food can help improve our overall health and well-being.</p>
